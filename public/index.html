How to Play

  • Words fall on your side — type the first letter to target, then finish it
  • The height when you finish determines which floor gets hit
  • Aim for already-damaged floors to break through faster
  • Three attack types based on word length (see legend in-game)
  • Solo: one wrong key and the word is gone — no second chances
